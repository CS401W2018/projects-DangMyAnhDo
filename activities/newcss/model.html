Questions:

1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags: take up the full width and start on a new line.

Inline tags: only take up the space they need and stay on the same line

2. What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin: space outside the element.

Border: the line around the element.

Padding: space inside the element.

3. What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

Px: pixels, a fixed size

%: percentage, relative to the parents.

em: based on the font size.

4. When would you use a figure tag?

We use the figure tag to group images or other media with a caption.
