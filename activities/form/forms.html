Questions:

1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

The "action" attribute tells where to send the form data

2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

The "method" attribute tells how: "GET" adds data to the URL, and "POST" sends it secretly.

3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

"GET" shows data in the URL, while "POST" keeps it hidden.
