Questions

1. What should you focus on during class and after class?

During class: I try to stay focused on what the professor is saying and take good notes, I ask something if i do not understand something, I focus and avoid distractions.

After class: I go over my notes to remember what we learned, I practice what we did in class, if i am stuck, I look for answers or ask TAs.

2. What is the main difference between CSS Grid and CSS Flex?

CSS Grid helps us arrange things in both rows and columns, like a whole page layout.

CSS Flex arranges things inn a rows or columns, good for small sections, it helps space things out evenly.
