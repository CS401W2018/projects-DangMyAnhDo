Final Project Proposal: A Taste of Vietnam

1. Title & Topic

"A Taste of Vietnam: Exploring Regional Vietnamese Cuisine"

This website will introduce the diverse flavors of Vietnamese food, from street food staples to regional specialties, while highlighting their cultural significance.

2. Why This Topic?

As a proud Vietnamese, I want to showcase the richness and regional diversity of our cuisine, which is often simplified to just pho and banh mi. I want to showcase its regional diversity (Northern, Central, Southern styles), cooking techniques, and lesser-known dishes. Food is a big part of Vietnamese culture, and this project will help others appreciate its depth beyond the basics.
